### Resources

- Resource Server can add or remove available resources
- Resource is named res://rs_public_id/path_or_uri

- Team leader can create team
- Team leader can add user to team
- Team leader can add resources to team
- Team leader can define a role (editor or observer) for each user in team
- Team leader can override the role for one user on one resource
